Thanksgiving Churros

Return Home


Celebrate Thanksgiving with a sweet twist by making these delectable Thanksgiving Churros. They are the perfect dessert to add a touch of festivity to your holiday meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon (for dusting)
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar (for dusting)
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, combine water and butter. Bring to a boil, then remove from heat.
  2. Stir in flour, salt, and 1/4 teaspoon of ground cinnamon until the mixture forms a ball.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time until the dough is smooth.
  4. Heat oil in a deep fryer or a large, deep pot to 375°F (190°C).
  5. Spoon the churro dough into a piping bag fitted with a large star tip.
  6. Pipe 6-inch long strips of dough into the hot oil. Use scissors to cut the dough. Fry until golden brown.
  7. Combine 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon and 1/4 cup of sugar. Roll the hot churros in the cinnamon-sugar mixture.
  8. Serve your Thanksgiving Churros warm and enjoy!
