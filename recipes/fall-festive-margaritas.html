Fall Festive Margaritas

Return Home

Enjoy the flavors of fall with this festive margarita recipe. The combination of cinnamon and apple makes it a perfect drink for the season.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Tequila
  • 1 oz Cinnamon Schnapps
  • 1 oz Triple Sec
  • 2 oz Apple Cider
  • 1 oz Fresh Lime Juice
  • 1/2 oz Simple Syrup (adjust to taste)
  • Cinnamon sticks and apple slices for garnish

Instructions

  1. Fill a shaker with ice.
  2. Add tequila, cinnamon schnapps, triple sec, apple cider, lime juice, and simple syrup to the shaker.
  3. Shake well until all ingredients are combined and the mixture is chilled.
  4. Strain the margarita mixture into a salt-rimmed glass filled with ice.
  5. Garnish with a cinnamon stick and a slice of apple.
  6. Serve and enjoy your fall festive margarita!
